The Mask Podcast

We started this podcast to share stories from both the people on the frontlines and behind the scenes of the COVID-19 pandemic. We speak anonymously to first responders, medical professionals, scientists, police officers, hospital custodians, and many more to learn what it’s really like to face this invisible enemy. By telling their personal stories, we hope to inspire our listeners to effect positive change during this horrible epidemic, now and in the future. This podcast, created by frequent collaborators and Academy Award-winning filmmakers Damon Russell and Andrew Napier, also serves as a living diary of this unprecedented time. Damon Russell - Creator / Director / Executive Producer

Damon is an award-winning director and producer. He directed the feature film SNOW ON THA BLUFF (Netflix) and produced the Oscar-winning short film CURFEW. He has also produced shows for National Geographic, Discovery, and A&E. Damon was named one of The 25 New Faces of Independent Film by Filmmaker Magazine and is a member of The Academy of Motion Picture Arts and Sciences.

Andrew Napier - Creator / Director / Executive Producer

Andrew is a director, writer and visual artist. He directed the award-winning documentary MAD AS HELL and at the age of 22 produced the Oscar-winning short film CURFEW. He was an early director and producer of the online show THE YOUNG TURKS. In 2019 he edited and executive produced the acclaimed film ACTIVE MEASURES.
